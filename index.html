Why Rapture Twelve

At Rapture Twelve, we operate with the precision and patience of advanced persistent threats, studying, adapting, and striking before risks turn into incidents. Our work blends offensive security expertise with advanced AI and machine learning; we simulate real-world adversaries, build intelligent detection systems, automate complex SOC operations, and engineer custom security tools that evolve alongside the threat landscape. Every solution we deliver, from covert red team operations to autonomous incident response, is designed to think like an attacker, outsmart them at every turn, and protect what matters most.
